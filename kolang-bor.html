Kolang Bor

Kolang Bor (Persian: كلنگبر) is a village in Bijnavand Rural District, in the Zagros District of Chardavol County, Ilam Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 302, in 66 families. The village is populated by Kurds.
