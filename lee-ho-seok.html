Lee Ho-seok

Jeon Seok-ho (Korean: 전석호; born May 2, 1984) is a South Korean actor. He is known for his role in Kingdom as the incompetent magistrate. He starred in TV series such as Misaeng: Incomplete Life (2014), The Good Wife (2016), and Strong Woman Do Bong-soon (2017).





== Early life and education ==

Jeon was born on May 2, 1984. Jeon first appeared on the screen in a minor role in the 2000 film Just Do It. In 2001, he appeared on the small screen in the EBS drama School Story. In 2003, he entered the Department of Theater and Film at Hanyang University. He said, "I fell in love with theater after meeting good professors and good seniors." He knew then that he wanted to do this for the rest of his life.





== Career ==





=== Early career in Travel series ===

Jeon, actors Park Dong-wook and Director Park Seon-hee, three alumni of Hanyang University's Theater Department left for India in February 2010 and traveled for a month. Upon returning, they were jointly planning the 'India Blog'.
