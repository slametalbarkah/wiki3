Khatamabad, Kermanshah

Khatamabad (Persian: خاتم اباد, also Romanized as Khātamābād) is a village in Bavaleh Rural District, in the Central District of Sonqor County, Kermanshah Province, Iran. According to the 2006 census, its population was 214, in 47 families.
