KF Ulpiana

KF Ulpiana (Albanian: Klubi Futbollistik Ulpiana Lipjan) is a professional football club based in Lipjan, Kosovo.[a] The club plays in the second division of football in Kosovo, Football Superleague of Kosovo.

The club plays its home games at the Sami Kelmendi Stadium, which has a capacity of 2,500, it does not meet the criteria of the Union of European Football Associations.
